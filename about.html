About Wayne AI Group

We perform cutting-edge AI research and offer world-class AI education.

The Wayne AI group was started by faculty members from the department of computer science in year 2020, with collaboration from other engineering departments, such as electric and computer engineering, civil engineering, and industrial engineering, college of liberal arts and science, and college of medicine across the campus.
